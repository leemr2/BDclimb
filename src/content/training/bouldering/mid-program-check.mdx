## Halfway through the cycle

Week 8 is the program midpoint and the second major retest week. You have six weeks of training behind you and the performance mesocycle ahead. This is the best time to read your data honestly.

## Comparing Week 0 vs Week 8

Look at max hang load, campus reach rung, and benchmark send rate side by side. Strength and power metrics may improve at different rates — that's normal. What matters is the trend, not matching someone else's numbers.

## Adjusting expectations

If max hang jumped but send rate lagged, Mesocycle 3 will emphasize transfer to the wall. If both moved together, you can push limit attempts with confidence. Flat metrics suggest reviewing recovery, load progression, or finger health before increasing intensity.

## Preparing for Mesocycle 3

Use this deload week to recover and interpret retest data. Mesocycle 3 shifts toward performance — hard sends, benchmark reattempts, and consolidating everything you've built. Clear mid-program data makes that phase more targeted.
